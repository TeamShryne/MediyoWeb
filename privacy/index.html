Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

This policy explains what Mediyo stores locally on your device and what is sent to third-party services required for app features.

Data Collection

Mediyo does not operate its own analytics backend. The app stores settings and session-related data locally on your device, and communicates with YouTube services to provide playback and account features.

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share data with third parties

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Login session data: Stored locally on your device so the app can access your YouTube Music account
  • Downloaded music: Cached locally for offline playback
  • App preferences: Settings and configurations stored locally
  • Playback history: Stored locally for your convenience

You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the application.

Third-Party Services

Mediyo connects to YouTube Music to provide its functionality. When you use Mediyo:

  • Your interactions with YouTube Music are subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Authentication is handled directly between your device and YouTube's servers
  • The app stores session-related account data locally to keep you signed in

Permissions

Mediyo requires the following Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: To stream music from YouTube Music
  • Storage Access: To download and cache music for offline playback
  • Network State: To detect connectivity and optimize streaming

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection.

Open Source

Mediyo is open source software. You can review our code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Mediyo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for users who meet YouTube Music's age requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
