FAQ

Is Mediyo free?

Yes, Mediyo is completely free and open source.

Is my data safe?

Mediyo stores account session data locally on your device and does not run analytics in the app. Like most local app storage, this is not a guarantee against all device-level threats.

Does it work offline?

Yes, you can download songs and listen to them offline without an internet connection.

What Android version is required?

Mediyo requires Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or higher.

How do I report bugs or issues?

You can report bugs and issues on our GitHub repository's issue tracker.
